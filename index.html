Bunkulator – KTU Attendance Calculator & Bunk Calculator

Bunkulator is a free KTU attendance calculator that tells you exactly how many classes you can bunk or skip while staying above your required attendance percentage. Used by college students across Kerala to plan their bunks smartly.

How to use the attendance calculator

Set your total number of classes, the classes you have attended, and your target attendance percentage (default 75% for KTU). Bunkulator instantly shows your current attendance, safe bunks remaining, or how many more classes you must attend.

KTU Attendance Rules

APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University requires 75% minimum attendance per subject. Missing more classes than Bunkulator safe limit risks losing exam eligibility.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many classes can I bunk?

Enter your attended and total classes in Bunkulator. The bunk calculator will show you the exact number of classes you can safely skip.

How to calculate attendance percentage?

Attendance percentage equals attended classes divided by total classes multiplied by 100. Bunkulator calculates this instantly and predicts future scenarios.

What if my attendance is below 75%?

Bunkulator will show how many consecutive classes you need to attend to recover and cross the 75% threshold.
